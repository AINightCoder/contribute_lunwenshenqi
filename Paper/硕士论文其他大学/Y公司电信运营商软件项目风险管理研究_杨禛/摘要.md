# Y公司电信运营商软件项目风险管理研究

# 摘要

软件项目风险管理的目标在于利用有限资源来最大限度地降低软件项目研发和管理过程中不确定性事件的发生概率，同时减轻这些潜在事件可能对项目造成的不利影响，从而提升软件项目的成功概率。然而，随着社会的发展，项目内外部环境也在不断变化，各种风险因素难以被有效识别。如何消除或有效控制项目风险，并进行有效的风险规划、识别、估计、评价、应对和监控，是项目成功的关键。

Y公司是一家专注移动互联网 $^ +$ 云计算的高科技企业，主要从事系统解决方案及软件服务开发的业务。本研究以Y公司电信运营商软件项目为例，考察项目风险管理的相关问题。结合软件行业发展的特点以及相关的文献资料，收集并分析了企业和项目的特性以及内外部环境的动态要素，以便有效地识别风险因素。风险识别阶段发现，在Y公司电信运营商中存在潜在的诸如技术和质量风险、人力资源和管理风险、需求和沟通风险、项目计划风险、外部环境风险等影响项目成功的风险因素。进一步的风险评估阶段揭示，主要的风险因素是技术和质量风险、人力资源和管理风险以及需求和沟通风险。运用层次分析和模糊综合评价分析发现，Y公司电信运营商软件项目整体风险等级为较高，各个二级指标除了项目计划风险属于一般风险外，其余均属于较高风险。

鉴于这些风险识别和评估结果，本研究提出了针对Y公司电信运营商软件项目风险应对的解决措施。包括对技术和质量风险应对，提高技术能力水平，建立统一的技术开发标准，规范代码审查，选择合适的项目负责人。对人力资源和管理风险应对，提升组织建设水平，增加人员培训，增强项目凝聚力，管理方面突出架构作用，提高技术能力水平。对需求和沟通风险应对，明确客户需求，严控需求变更，加强人员沟通。对项目计划风险应对，制定完善的项目管理计划，强化分包商管理。对外部环境风险应对，提高工作人员积极性，拓展市场，推动多元化发展。

本研究通过对Y公司电信运营商软件项目的风险识别和评估，从而提出有效的应对措施，有益于项目执行过程中的风险控制，为其他电信运营商软件项目提供借鉴和参考，有重要的实践价值和理论指导意义。

关键词：风险管理；模糊综合评价法；风险应对；软件项目

# Research on Software Project Risk Management for Telecom Operators of Company Y

# Abstract

The goal of software project risk management is to utilize limited resources to minimize the probability of uncertain events occurring during the development and management process of software projects, while also mitigating the potential adverse effects of these events on the project, thereby improving the success probability of software projects. However, with the development of society, the internal and external environment of projects is constantly changing, and various risk factors are difficult to effectively identify. How to eliminate or effectively control project risks, and carry out effective risk planning, identification, estimation, evaluation, response, and monitoring, is the key to project success.

Y Company is a high-tech enterprise focusing on mobile Internet plus+cloud computing, mainly engaged in the development of system solutions and software services. This study takes the software project of Y Company's telecommunications operator as an example to examine the relevant issues of project risk management. Based on the characteristics of the development of the software industry and relevant literature, we collected and analyzed the characteristics of enterprises and projects, as well as the dynamic elements of the internal and external environment, in order to effectively identify risk factors. During the risk identification stage, it was discovered that there are potential risk factors in Y Company's telecommunications operator that may affect project success, such as technical and quality risks, human resources and management risks, demand and communication risks, project planning risks, external environmental risks, etc. The further risk assessment stage reveals that the main risk factors are technical and quality risks, human resources and management risks, and demand and communication risks. Using Analytic Hierarchy Process and Fuzzy Comprehensive Evaluation, it was found that the overall risk level of Y Company's telecommunications operator software project is relatively high. Except for the project plan risk, all other secondary indicators belong to higher risks.

Based on these risk identification and assessment results, this study proposes solutions for the risk response of Y Company's telecommunications operator software project. This includes responding to technical and quality risks, improving technical capabilities, establishing unified technical development standards, standardizing code reviews, and selecting appropriate project leaders. To respond to human resources and management risks, improve organizational construction level, increase personnel training, enhance project cohesion, highlight the role of architecture in management, and improve technical capabilities. Respond to requirements and communication risks, clarify customer needs, strictly control changes in requirements, and strengthen personnel communication. Respond to project plan risks, develop a comprehensive project management plan, and strengthen subcontractor management. Respond to external environmental risks, increase staff motivation, expand the market, and promote diversified development.

This study identifies and evaluates the risks of Y company's telecommunications operator software project, and proposes effective response measures, which are beneficial for risk control during project execution. It provides reference and guidance for other telecommunications operator software projects, and has important practical value and theoretical guidance significance.

Key words: Risk management; Fuzzy comprehensive evaluation method; Risk response; software project
