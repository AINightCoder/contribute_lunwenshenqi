# 摘 要

中小微企业是我国经济中的重要参与者，但因各种客观的原因，长久以来中小微企业的生存都尤为艰难。作为我国社会发展和经济增长重要的推动力量，中小微企业在其各自的领域已然起到了不可替代的作用，但是由于其存有资产规模小、内部的治理机制缺失、财务信息尚不透明之类的问题，各大银行在进行贷款的审批时考虑到信贷风险，对其融资保持谨慎态度。同时，由于我国企业征信体系的不完善，中小微企业寿命又普遍偏短，银行与其“信息不对称”问题十分严重，因此我国的小微企业长期面对融资难题。在近几年中，中小微企业的现金流紧张、营业额下降、回款周期变长等现象尤为突出，如何将金融支持与中小企业自身特质进行有机结合，筛选出真正优质有需求资金企业便成为了社会各界均高度关注的话题，而这也是本文所探讨的方向，希望通过完善微E贷产品帮助解决这部分问题。

在移动互联网以及大数据技术逐渐成熟的背景下，产品与模式正在快速革新。贷款的民营机构与商业银行的成立发展，使贷款机构与产品都呈现井喷式增加，但是监管与业务发展的不匹配，导致隐藏了大量的金融风险。PA银行作为全国领先的股份制银行，在支持三农、普惠中小微企业的大背景下，也推出了微 E 贷产品。微 E 贷产品是 PA 银行推出的主要针对中小微型企业、从事种植、养殖的农户及新型农业经营主体的自然人等的贷款产品。本文以PA银行微E 贷产品为研究对象，对于类似的产品进行风险类型的识别与评估，是其在风险管理理论与内部风险控制理论的基础上，并借鉴全球前沿的风险管理理论所得出的研究结果，希望得出较为合理的风险应对与控制措施。对比目前市面上现有信贷类产品，分析目前微E 贷存在的风险，对应分析产品内部的风险管理存在的问题并提出改进措施，最终实现降低微 E 贷产品的风险性，增加 PA 银行盈利的同时，更高效、便捷地为优质中小企业客户提供融资通道，助力中小企业更好的发展，为我国的经济发展做出属于 PA银行应有的贡献。

关键词：商业银行；中小微企业；信贷产品；微E贷

# Abstract

Small, medium and micro enterprises (SMMEs) are important participants in China's economy, but due to various objective reasons, their survival has been particularly difficult for a long time. As an important driving force for China's social development and economic growth, SMMEs have played an irreplaceable role in their respective fields. However, due to their small asset size, lack of internal governance mechanisms, and opaque financial information, major banks are cautious about financing them when considering credit risks during loan approval. Meanwhile, due to the imperfect credit system of Chinese enterprises and the relatively short life expectancy of SMMEs, the "information asymmetry" between banks and them is quite serious. Therefore, China's small and micro enterprises have long faced financing difficulties. In recent years, phenomena such as tight cash flow, declining sales revenue, and longer collection cycles of SMMEs have become particularly prominent. How to organically combine financial support with the unique characteristics of SMEs and screen out truly high-quality enterprises with financing needs has become a topic of great concern to all sectors of society, which is also the direction discussed in this paper. It hopes to help solve these problems by improving the Micro E-loan product.

Against the backdrop of the gradual maturity of mobile internet and big data technology, products and models are undergoing rapid innovation. The establishment and development of private lending institutions and commercial banks have led to a surge in lending institutions and products. However, the mismatch between supervision and business development has led to a large number of hidden financial risks. As a leading joint-stock bank in China, Bank PA has also launched the Micro E-loan product under the background of supporting agriculture, rural areas, farmers, and inclusive SMMEs. The Micro E-loan product is a loan product launched by Bank PA, mainly targeting small and medium-sized enterprises, farmers engaged in planting and breeding, and natural persons of new agricultural business entities. This paper takes Bank PA's Micro E-loan product as the research object and identifies and evaluates the risk types of similar products based on risk management theory and internal risk control theory, while referring to global advanced risk management theories. It hopes to obtain more reasonable risk response and control measures. By comparing existing credit products on the market, analyzing the current risks of Micro E-loan, analyzing the problems in the internal risk management of the product, and proposing improvement measures, it ultimately aims to reduce the risk of Micro E-loan products, increase Bank PA's profits, provide financing channels for high-quality SME customers more efficiently and conveniently, and help SMEs achieve better development, making due contributions to China's economic development as Bank PA.

Keywords: Commercial banks, micro enterprises, credit products, Micro-E loans.
