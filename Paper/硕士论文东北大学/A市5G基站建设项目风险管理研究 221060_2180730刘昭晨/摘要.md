# A 市 5G 基站建设项目风险管理研究

# 摘 要

第五代移动通信技术（5th Generation Mobile Communication Technology，简称 5G）是具有高速率、低时延和大连接特点的新一代宽带移动通信技术，5G 通讯设施是实现人机物互联的网络基础设施。如果不能正确的识别、评价和防范 A 市 5G 通信基站建设项目风险，很可能造成许多问题，比如由于项目风险识别的失误，会遗漏某些重要的风险因素，使建设企业无法全面的评估项目风险和困难，在项目实际实施中，轻则导致项目整体进度延后、质量下降，重则导致项目失败；若项目风险评价不达标，则建设企业无法有效的对多个风险因素进行分级，导致企业即使耗费大量人力物力执行防范措施，也无法做到对高损失风险因素进行准确预防，很有可能影响后期 5G通信基站功能目标实现。

本研究以 A 市 5G 基站建设项目为研究对象，运用风险管理相关理论与方法，首先，论文采用相似案例法和头脑风暴法初步识别了 A 市 5G 基站建设项目风险，初步整理了 A 市 5G 基站建设项目风险因素，基于德尔菲法对 A 市 5G 基站建设项目风险进行筛选和修正，并进行信度效度和重要性检验；其次，通过极值统计法计算出各个风险因素的发生概率，以及通过层次分析法计算出风险因素的风险综合权重，再计算出各项风险因素危害性在全部风险危害性总和中的占比，划分风险等级；最后，基于上述研究得出的风险评价结果，提出对应的风险防范和控制措施。

本文对 A 市 5G 基站建设项目的风险识别、风险评价和风险防范与控制研究，得到以下三点结论：(1) 通过风险识别确定了政策风险、社会环境风险、自然环境风险等六个风险分类，共 25 项项目风险因素；(2) 通过风险评估计算后，得出了 7 项 A 类风险因素风险危害的占比超过 $60 \%$ ，10 项 B 类风险因素风险危害累计占比超过 $30 \%$ ，8项 C 类风险因素风险危害的占比不到 $10 \%$ ；(3) 根据 A 类风险提出优先防范和主动控制措施，根据 B 类风险提出经常防范和常规控制措施，根据 C 类风险提出适当防范和宽松控制措施，为后续建设项目的正常推进奠定基础。同时，本文研究成果不仅为通讯建设企业对于 5G基站建设项目的风险分类和因素识别的完整性和合理性提供了整套识别流程；还为相关企业在项目风险评估中提出了科学的风险等级划分方法；给其它通讯建设相关企业在项目风险管理方面提供了一定的研究思路。

关键词：5G 基站；项目风险；风险识别；风险评价

# Research on Risk Management of 5G Base Station Construction Project in City A

# Abstract

The 5th mobile communication technology (5G for short) is a new generation of broadband mobile communication technology with the characteristics of high speed, low latency and large connection. 5G communication facilities are the network infrastructure that realizes the interconnection of humans, machines and things. If the risks of 5G communication base station construction projects cannot be correctly identified, evaluated and prevented, it is likely to cause many problems. For example, due to errors in project risk identification, some important risk factors will be missed, making it impossible for construction companies to comprehensively assess project risks and difficulties, in the actual implementation of the project, at least it will cause the overall project progress to be delayed and quality to decline, and at worst it will lead to project failure; if the project risk assessment does not meet the standards, the construction company will not be able to effectively classify multiple risk factors, this enables enterprises to implement preventive measures even if they spend a lot of manpower and financial resources., it is impossible to accurately prevent high loss risk factors, which is likely to affect the realization of the functional goals of 5G communication base stations in the future.

The research takes the construction project of 5G base stations in City A as the research object, and applies relevant theories and methods of risk management. Firstly, the paper uses the similarity case method and brainstorming method to preliminarily identify the risks of the construction project of 5G base stations in City A, and preliminarily organizes the risk factors of the construction project of 5G base stations in City A. Based on the Delphi method, the risks of the construction project of 5G base stations in City A are screened and corrected, and reliability, validity, and importance tests are conducted; Secondly, the probability of occurrence of each risk factor is calculated using extreme value statistics, and the comprehensive risk weight of each risk factor is calculated using Analytic Hierarchy Process. Then, the proportion of the harmfulness of each risk factor in the total risk harmfulness is calculated, and the risks level are divided; Finally, based on the risk assessment results obtained from the above research, corresponding risk prevention and control measures are proposed.

This article conducts research on risk identification, risk assessment, and risk prevention and control of the 5G base station construction project in City A. The following three conclusions are drawn: (1) Six risk classifications, including policy risk, social environmental risk, and natural environmental risk, were determined through risk identification, with a total of 25 project risk factors; (2) After conducting risk assessment calculations, it was found that 7 Aclass risk factors accounted for over $60 \%$ of the risk hazards, $1 0 ~ \mathrm { B }$ -class risk factors accounted for over $30 \%$ of the risk hazards, and $8 \mathrm { C }$ -class risk factors accounted for less than $10 \%$ of the risk hazards; (3) Propose priority prevention and proactive control measures based on Class A risks, regular prevention and routine control measures based on Class B risks, and appropriate prevention and lenient control measures based on Class C risks, laying a foundation for the normal progress of subsequent construction projects. At the same time, the research results of this article not only provide a complete identification process for the completeness and rationality of risk classification and factor identification of 5G base station construction projects for communication construction enterprises; A scientific risk level classification method has also been proposed for relevant enterprises in project risk assessment; The risk control methods proposed based on different levels can also provide effective references for similar project risk control in other companies, and provide certain research ideas for other communication construction related enterprises in project risk management.

Key words: 5G base station; project risk; risk identification; risk evaluation
