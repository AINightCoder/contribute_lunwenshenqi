# 摘 要

奉新SD选矿厂项目是中铁J局DL分公司在国内承建的第一个矿建项目。作为施工企业，随着公司对国内采矿项目建设领域的拓展，公司在国内行业管理经验不足的缺点暴露了出来。项目的施工风险是影响建设项目成败的关键性。奉新 SD 选矿厂项目采用的新技术、新工艺无疑对项目建设团队是一次前所未有的考验；另外由于新能源产品原材料的特殊性，奉新 SD 选矿厂项目对工期的要求也是格外的严格，这无形中又大大的增加项目的施工风险。为了保证项目的顺利进行并为之后的项目积累经验，有必要对 SD 选矿厂项目进行风险管理研究。

本文运用现代项目风险管理的理论与方法，通过对项目进行风险规划、风险识别、风险评估、风险应对、风险跟踪来对项目施工风险进行全面细致的研究。首先运用德尔菲法对选矿厂项目进行施工风险识别、之后运用层次分析法建立模型计算对应的施工风险的权重，在此基础上结合模糊分析法对当前施工风险进行量化评价，做出选矿厂项目的总体风险情况评价。结果显示，选矿厂项目当前施工风险处于中等风险的状态，需要采取措施加强管理。最后，针对不同的施工风险与对应的严重情况进行风险应对与风险跟踪。

本文的研究结论能够为奉新 SD 选矿厂项目的顺利实施与风险管理工作提供有效的参考，同时风险分析预评价过程积累的经验和教训也可以在将来为同类型的项目的风险管理提供参考，对公司在相关项目的投标竞争有着很大的支撑作用。

关键词：风险规划；风险识别；风险评估；风险应对；模糊分析法

# Abstract

Fengxin SD concentrator project is the first mine construction project undertaken by DL Branch of China Railway J Bureau in China. As a construction enterprise, with the company's expansion of the construction field of domestic mining projects, the company's shortcomings in the domestic industry management experience have been exposed. The construction risk of a project is the key to the success or failure of a construction project. The new technology and new process adopted by the Fengxin SD concentrator project are undoubtedly an unprecedented test for the project construction team; In addition, due to the particularity of raw materials for new energy products, the requirements for the construction period of the Fengxin SD concentrator project are also extremely strict, which virtually greatly increases the construction risk of the project. In order to ensure the smooth progress of the project and to gain experience for subsequent projects, it is necessary to conduct a risk management study on the SD concentrator project.

This paper uses the theories and methods of modern project risk management to conduct a comprehensive and detailed study of project construction risks through risk planning, risk identification, risk assessment, risk response and risk tracking. Firstly, the Delphi method is used to identify the construction risk of the concentrator project, and then the analytic hierarchy process is used to establish a model to calculate the weight of the corresponding construction risk, and then the current construction risk is quantitatively evaluated by the fuzzy analysis method, and the overall risk evaluation of the concentrator project is made. The results show that the current construction risk of the concentrator project is in a state of moderate risk, and measures need to be taken to strengthen management. Finally, risk response and risk tracking are carried out for different construction risks and corresponding serious situations.

The research conclusions of this paper can provide an effective reference for the smooth implementation and risk management of the Fengxin SD concentrator project, and the experience and lessons accumulated in the risk analysis and pre-evaluation process can also provide a reference for the risk management of similar projects in the future, which has a great supporting role in the bidding competition of related projects.

Key words: risk planning; risk identification; Risk assessment; risk response; fuzzy analysis
