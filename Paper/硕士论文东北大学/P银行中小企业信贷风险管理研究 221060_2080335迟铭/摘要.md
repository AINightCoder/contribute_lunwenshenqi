# 摘 要

随着改革开放的不断深化，我国经济快速发展，许多中小企业应运而生，为我国经济发展和扩大就业作出了重要贡献。但随着国际金融危机的爆发，市场环境活跃度降低，中小企业经营管理规范性差、抗风险能力低，亏损比例高，违约现象多，还贷能力弱的特征不断显现出来。由于中小企业自身特征，我国商业银行为中小企业提供贷款过程中，积累了大量不良贷款，利润受到影响。因此，商业银行如何加强信贷风险管理，降低信贷风险成为亟须解决的问题。作为国有控股的商业银行之一的 P 银行，为中小企业发展和国家经济社会繁荣做出了重要的贡献，但在中小企业信贷风险管理方面面临巨大挑战，尚存在诸多问题和不足。鉴于此，本文以 P 银行中小企业信贷风险管理为研究对象，试图为 P 银行提供一套有效的中小企业信贷业务风险管理体系，使其能够降低风险水平，提高资产质量，更好地为中小企业和社会发展服务，并实现自身的可持续、创新型发展。

本文选取 P 银行为研究对象，依据相关的管理理论、实践、法规、政策和现有的研究成果，以风险识别、风险评估、风险应对与控制为研究内容，运用德尔菲法、层次分析法和模糊评估法具体分析 P 银行中小企业信贷风险管理方面存在的问题和不足，研究发现：（1）P 银行中小企业信贷流程中存在的不足是贷前调查维度不全面，贷中审查审批效率较低，员工素质不高，贷后管理及资金流向监管不严格；（2）P 银行中小企业信贷风险管理中存在的主要问题是信用评级方法单一且落后、风险点识别不到位及风险控制措施不完善；（3）P 银行面临的主要信贷风险类型包括信用风险、操作风险和市场风险；（4）影响其信用风险的最关键因素是对于借款企业现金流把控不够，影响其操作风险的最关键因素是银行内控制度不够完善，影响其市场风险的最关键因素是他行信贷政策的变化。

最后，对于P 银行面对的不同类型信贷风险提出相应的应对策略建议。针对信用风险 P 银行需重视中小企业信贷客户的非财务信息、注重对中小型企业贷后管理过程的动态化、建立中小型企业信贷违约信息及时通报制度；针对操作风险，P 银行需完善风险管理部门架构、保证合规部门专家组成员的相对独立性；针对市场风险，P 银行需建立适合中小企业授信调查的科学管理模式、丰富中小企业授信业务抵质押担保手段。

关键词：中小企业，商业银行，信贷业务，风险管理

# Abstract

With the continuous deepening of reform and opening up, China's economy has developed rapidly, and many small and medium-sized enterprises have emerged, making important contributions to China's economic development and expanding employment. But with the outbreak of the international financial crisis, the activity of the market environment has decreased, and small and medium-sized enterprises have poor regulatory management, low risk resistance, high proportion of losses, multiple default phenomena, and weak loan repayment ability. Due to the unique characteristics of small and medium-sized enterprises, commercial banks in China have accumulated a large amount of non-performing loans during the process of providing loans to these enterprises, which has affected their profits. Therefore, how commercial banks can strengthen credit risk management and reduce credit risk has become an urgent problem that needs to be solved. As one of the state-owned commercial banks, P Bank has made important contributions to the development of small and medium-sized enterprises and the prosperity of the national economy and society. However, it faces huge challenges in credit risk management for small and medium-sized enterprises, and there are still many problems and shortcomings. In view of this, this article takes P Bank's small and medium-sized enterprise credit risk management as the research object, attempting to provide an effective small and medium-sized enterprise credit business risk management system for P Bank, so that it can reduce risk levels, improve asset quality, better serve the development of small and medium-sized enterprises and society, and achieve its own sustainable and innovative development.

This paper selects Bank $\mathrm { P }$ as the research object, based on relevant management theories, practices, regulations, policies and existing research results, takes risk identification, risk assessment, risk response and control as the research content, and uses Delphi method, analytic hierarchy process and fuzzy evaluation method to specifically analyze the problems and shortcomings in the credit risk management of Bank P's SMEs. The research finds that: (1) The shortcomings in the credit process of small and medium-sized enterprises in $\mathrm { P }$ Bank include incomplete investigation dimension before loan, low efficiency of examination and approval during loan, low quality of staff, and lax supervision of post-loan management and capital flow; (2) The main problems in $\mathrm { P }$ Bank's credit risk management for small and medium-sized enterprises are the single and outdated credit rating methods, inadequate identification of risk points, and incomplete risk control measures; (3) Credit risk, operational risk, and market risk are the main types of credit risks faced by P Bank; (4) The most critical factor affecting its credit risk is insufficient control over the cash flow of borrowing enterprises, the most critical factor affecting its operational risk is the imperfect internal control system of banks, and the most critical factor affecting its market risk is changes in credit policies of other banks.

Finally, this article proposes corresponding response and control strategies for different types of credit risks faced by P Bank. In response to credit risk, banks need to pay attention to the non-financial information of small and medium-sized enterprise credit customers, pay attention to the dynamic management process of small and medium-sized enterprise post loan, and establish a timely reporting system for credit default information of small and medium-sized enterprises; Regarding operational risks, P Bank needs to improve the structure of its risk management department and ensure the relative independence of the members of the compliance department's expert group; In response to market risks, P Bank needs to establish a scientific management model suitable for credit investigation of small and medium-sized enterprises, and enrich the collateral and guarantee methods for credit business of small and medium-sized enterprises.

Key words: small and Medium-sized enterprises, commercial banks, credit business, risk management
