# 摘 要

随着经济的发展和金融市场的日益完善，普惠型小微企业在国家经济中的作用逐渐凸显，它们获得有效的融资支持是经济发展的重要保障。S银行作为一家具有社会责任的银行，一直致力于为普惠型小微企业提供授信服务。然而，普惠型小微企业规模较小、抗风险能力弱、易受环境影响，尤其在新冠疫情的影响下，普惠型小微企业违约情况大幅提升，其信用风险不断增加，这对 S银行的信用风险管理提出了新的挑战。

本文以S银行普惠型小微企业授信业务为研究对象，通过信用风险识别、风险评估、风险控制的风险管理过程，对该行普惠型小微企业的信用风险管理问题进行研究。首先，整理普惠型小微企业定义及信用风险相关基础理论；其次，分析该行普惠型小微企业信贷业务现状及授信业务管理存在的问题；再次，通过问卷调查法和信息价值统计量识别出影响普惠型小微企业授信业务信用风险的重要因素；然后，利用 Logistic 回归构建信用评级模型，评估普惠型小微企业授信业务信用风险；最后，结合风险评估结果和银行自身经营存在的问题，从行业趋势分析、银企信息对称、企业经营管理、实际控制人资信等八个方面提出控制普惠型小微企业信用风险的应对措施。

研究结果显示，本文构建的信用评级模型能够较好地评估S银行普惠型小微企业的信用风险，提升 S 银行的信用风险管理能力，对普惠型小微企业授信业务的健康发展具有积极的作用，也为其他商业银行在普惠型小微企业信用风险管理方面提供参考和启示。

关键词：风险管理，普惠型小微企业，信用风险，信用评级

# Abstract

With the development of the economy and the increasing improvement of financial markets, the role of inclusive small and micro enterprises in the national economy is gradually becoming prominent. Their effective financing support is an important guarantee for economic development. As a socially responsible bank, S Bank has always been committed to providing credit services for inclusive small and micro enterprises. However, inclusive small and micro enterprises are small in scale, weak in risk resistance and vulnerable to environmental impact. Especially under the impact of the COVID-19, the default situation of inclusive small and micro enterprises has increased significantly, and their credit risk has continued to increase, which poses a new challenge to S Bank's credit risk management.

This article takes S Bank's inclusive small and micro enterprise credit business as the research object, and studies the credit risk management issues of S Bank's inclusive small and micro enterprises through the risk management process of credit risk identification, risk assessment, and risk control. Firstly, organize the definition of inclusive small and micro enterprises and basic theories related to credit risk; Secondly, analyze the current situation of the bank's inclusive small and micro enterprise credit business and the problems in credit management; Once again, important factors affecting the credit risk of inclusive small and micro enterprise credit business were identified through questionnaire survey and information value statistics; Then, use logistic regression to construct a credit rating model to evaluate the credit risk of inclusive small and micro enterprise credit business; Finally, based on the risk assessment results and the existing problems in the bank's own operations, countermeasures are proposed to control the credit risk of inclusive small and micro enterprises from eight aspects: industry trend analysis, information symmetry between banks and enterprises, enterprise management, and actual controller credit.

The research results show that the credit rating model constructed in this article can effectively evaluate the credit risk of S Bank's inclusive small and micro enterprises, enhance S Bank's credit risk management ability, and have a positive effect on the healthy development of inclusive small and micro enterprise credit business. It also provides reference and inspiration for other commercial banks in the credit risk management of inclusive small and micro enterprises.

Key words: risk management, inclusive small and micro enterprises, credit risk, credit rating
