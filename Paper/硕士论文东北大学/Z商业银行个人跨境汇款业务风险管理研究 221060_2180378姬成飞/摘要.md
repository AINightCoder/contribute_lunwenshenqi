# 摘 要

伴随我国加快构建“双循环”新发展格局、全面提高对外开放水平以及资本市场双向资金流动的加快，境内外个人参与国际经济活动呈上升趋势，除经常项目业务外，资本项目相关的个人需求也日益凸显。从监管角度，我国不断深化外汇管理改革；从实践角度，个人外汇业务呈现高额低频、综合收益贡献高、区域特色突出等特征，但也因行业监管要求严格、案例个体差异大、背景审核难度高、营销联动性强等特点，对受理银行的专业度要求较高。

Z商业银行依托覆盖全球的服务网络和丰富的国际业务经验，整合集团多元化产品和服务，面向有跨境金融服务需求的各类人群推出“Z商业银行出国金融服务”，为客户提供便捷周到的一站式跨境金融服务。

本文以Z商业银行近年来遇到的实际问题及案例为研究对象，以风险管理理论为支撑进行深入分析。首先通过Z 商业银行个人跨境汇款业务面临的问题，识别个人跨境汇款业务中存在的各种风险因素。其次，使用层次分析法、模糊综合评价法对个人跨境汇款业务的风险指标进行比较和权重分配，得出各指标的相对重要性。随之，将风险程度划分为高、中、低等级，以直观方式反映风险程度。最后，针对不同风险等级，提出相应的风险控制方案，帮助Z 商业银行改进个人跨境汇款业务的风险管理水平。

本文针对Z商业银行个人跨境汇款业务的风险管理研究具有理论与应用的双重价值。在理论层面：对Z 商业银行个人跨境汇款业务的风险管理进行了科学的分析，通过对风险管理模型的应用，进一步强化了风险管理理论的基础，从而促进了对个人跨境汇款业务风险管理的理论发展。在应用层面：通过提供具体的风险控制对策，为Z 商业银行在个人跨境汇款业务中的风险管理提供了实际操作的指导，增强了业务的可操作性，制定风险管理策略为商业银行的决策者提供了有力的支持，有望降低由各类风险带来的经济损失，有助于提高Z 商业银行个人跨境金融业务的可持续发展。

关键词：商业银行；跨境汇款业务；风险识别；风险评估；风险控制

# Abstract

With China accelerating the construction of a new development pattern of "double cycle", comprehensively improving the level of opening up to the outside world, and accelerating the two-way capital flow in the capital market, the participation of domestic and foreign individuals in international economic activities is on the rise. In addition to current account business, the personal needs related to capital projects are also increasingly prominent. From a regulatory perspective, China is continuously deepening its foreign exchange management reform; From a practical perspective, personal foreign exchange business presents characteristics such as high and low frequency, high contribution to comprehensive income, and prominent regional characteristics. However, due to strict industry regulatory requirements, large individual differences in cases, high difficulty in background review, and strong marketing linkage, there is a high demand for the professionalism of the accepting bank.

Based on its global service network and rich international business experience, Z Commercial Bank integrates the group's diversified products and services, and launches "Z Commercial Bank Overseas Financial Services" to various groups of people with cross-border financial service needs, providing customers with convenient and thoughtful one-stop cross-border financial services.

This paper takes the practical problems and cases encountered by Z Commercial Bank in recent years as the research object, and conducts in-depth analysis supported by risk management theory. Firstly, identify various risk factors in personal cross-border remittance business through the problems faced by Z Commercial Bank's personal cross-border remittance business. Secondly, the Analytic Hierarchy Process and Fuzzy Comprehensive Evaluation Method are used to compare and assign weights to the risk indicators of individual cross-border remittance business, and the relative importance of each indicator is obtained. Subsequently, the risk level is divided into high, medium, and low levels to intuitively reflect the risk level. Finally, corresponding risk control plans are proposed for different risk levels to help Z

Commercial Bank improve its risk management level in personal cross-border remittance business.

This paper focuses on the risk management research of Z Commercial Bank's personal cross-border remittance business, which has both theoretical and practical value. At the theoretical level, a scientific analysis was conducted on the risk management of Z Commercial Bank's personal cross-border remittance business. Through the application of risk management models, the foundation of risk management theory was further strengthened, thereby promoting the theoretical development of risk management for personal cross-border remittance business. At the application level, by providing specific risk control measures, practical guidance has been provided for Z Commercial Bank's risk management in personal cross-border remittance business, enhancing the operability of the business, and formulating risk management strategies has provided strong support for decision-makers of the commercial bank. It is expected to reduce economic losses caused by various risks and help improve the sustainable development of Z Commercial Bank's personal cross-border remittance business.

Key words: commercial bank; cross-border remittance business; risk identification; risk assessment; risk management
