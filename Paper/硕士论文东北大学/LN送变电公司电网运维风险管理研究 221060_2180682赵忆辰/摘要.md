# 摘 要

电力能源是国民经济和社会发展的重要物质基础。输电线路作为电力系统中重要的组成部分，在输电过程中承担着重要角色。由于电网运行的复杂性、运维管理的特殊性以及外部环境因素的影响，输电线路在运行过程中存在较多不确定因素，导致电网运维面临诸多风险。科学识别、评估和管控电网运维风险，对提升电网运维质量、保障电力系统安全稳定运行具有重要意义。但受管理理念、管理手段等因素制约，电网企业在运维风险管理方面还存在不足。如何全面识别电网运维风险，科学评估风险等级，提出有针对性的风险管控措施，已成为电网企业亟需解决的关键问题。

本文基于风险管理相关理论，以LN 送变电公司为研究对象，结合其电网运维风险管理现状，采用头脑风暴法和流程图分析法对电网运维风险进行识别。在此基础上，运用德尔菲法对风险因素进行初步评价，继而构建层次分析法和模糊综合评价相结合的风险评价模型，并应用该模型对风险开展综合评估。最后，根据风险评估结果提出相应的风险应对和控制措施。

研究发现，LN 送变电公司电网运维总体风险处于“较低”到“中等”水平，但外包管理、应急管理、自然灾害等领域风险较为突出，相关管控措施有待进一步细化完善。要从强化责任落实、优化规划设计、动态风险辨识、智能运维体系建设、应急管理能力提升等方面为着力点和出发点，健全风险管理的组织体系、制度保障和技术支撑，强化各专业条线、各管控环节的协同联动，构建全过程、全方位、全天候的电网运维风险防控体系。

关键词：电网企业；运维管理；风险评估；风险控制

# Abstract

The electric power energy is an important material foundation for national economic and social development. As a crucial component of the power system, transmission lines play a vital role in the process of power transmission. Due to the complexity of power grid operation, the particularity of operation and maintenance management, and the influence of external environmental factors, there exist many uncertainties in the operation of transmission lines, resulting in numerous risks faced by power grid operation and maintenance. Scientific identification, assessment and control of operation and maintenance risks are of great significance to improving the quality of power grid operation and maintenance and ensuring the safe and stable operation of the power system. However, constrained by management philosophy, management means and other factors, there are still deficiencies in the operation and maintenance risk management of power grid enterprises. How to comprehensively identify the operation and maintenance risks of power grid, scientifically assess the risk level, and propose targeted risk control measures have become key issues that power grid enterprises urgently need to solve.

Based on risk management theories and taking LN Power Transmission and Transformation Company as the research object, this paper identifies the risks in power grid operation and maintenance (O&M) through brainstorming and flow chart analysis, in combination with the current status of the company's risk management practices. On this basis, the Delphi method is employed for preliminary risk factor evaluation. Furthermore, an integrated risk assessment model combining Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP) and Fuzzy Comprehensive Evaluation is constructed and applied. Finally, corresponding risk response and control measures are proposed based on the assessment results, aiming to provide theoretical basis and practical reference for enhancing the company's power grid O&M risk management.

The research finds that the overall risk level of LN Company's power grid O&M is "low" to "medium", but risks in areas such as outsourcing management, emergency management, and natural disasters are relatively prominent, requiring further refinement and improvement of related control measures. The paper suggests that efforts should be made to strengthen responsibility implementation, optimize planning and design, conduct dynamic risk identification, build intelligent O&M systems, and enhance emergency management capabilities. The goal is to improve the organizational structure, institutional guarantee and technical support for risk management, reinforce coordination among different professional lines and control links, and strive to build a whole-process, all-round, all-weather risk prevention and control system for power grid O&M. The research outcomes are of great significance for promoting the high-quality development of LN Company's power grid O&M, and also provide insights for risk management practices of similar power grid enterprises.

Key words: power grid enterprise; operation and maintenance management; risk assessment; risk control
