# 摘 要

目前我国的房地产正步入新一轮调整期，政府部门持续发布针对楼市的宏观调控政策并对房地产项目贷款情况加强监管，旨在有效控制杠杆和楼市泡沫，推动房地产的良性发展，在“房住不炒”和“去杠杆”政策调控下，房地产行业景气度逐渐回落。考虑到YHJ项目具有体量大、周期长、高风险的特点，在实现高质量发展的目标下对于项目开发过程中各阶段的风险管理更显得尤为重要。

本文以风险管理相关理论及方法为指导对YHJ 项目开展研究。首先，对项目开发及风险管理基本概况进行了介绍。其次运用核对表法及头脑风暴法对YHJ项目开展两轮风险识别，筛选出在规划设计、施工建设、销售运营阶段主要风险影响因素并进行分析。基于层次分析法对风险识别阶段识别出的风险因素进行风险评估，构建YHJ项目风险指标体系及判断矩阵并进行风险等级排序，将其分为重大风险、较大风险、一般风险及低风险。最后对筛选出的风险因素制定相应应对措施，并建立风险监控及预警机制对 YHJ项目风险管理形成闭环。

本研究以 YHJ 项目为例，将风险管理理论与项目实际结合，总结经验并实现改进，形成闭环思维，对于项目后续开发过程的风险研判及风险应对具有借鉴意义。

关键词：房地产项目；风险管理；风险识别；风险评估；层次分析法

# Abstract

At present, China's real estate is entering a new adjustment period. Government departments continue to issue macro -control policies for the real estate market, and strengthen supervision of real estate project loans, aiming to effectively control the leverage and bubbles of the real estate market, and promote the healthy development of real estate. Under the control of unsurpted real estate and deleveraging policies, the prosperity of the real estate industry has gradually declined. Considering the characteristics of the YHJ project, such as large capacity, long cycle, and high risk, management risks in various stages of the project development process are particularly important to achieve the goals of high -quality development.

This article is guided by the theories and methods related to risk management to conduct research on the YHJ project. Firstly, the basic overview of project development and risk management was introduced. Secondly, the checklist method and brainstorming method were used to conduct two rounds of risk identification for the YHJ project, screening and analyzing the main risk influencing factors in the planning, design, construction, and sales operation stages. Based on the Analytic Hierarchy Process, risk assessment is conducted on the identified risk factors during the risk identification stage, and a YHJ project risk indicator system and judgment matrix are constructed. The risk levels are sorted and classified into major risks, major risks, general risks, and low risks. Finally, corresponding response measures should be developed for the selected risk factors, and a risk monitoring and early warning mechanism should be established to form a closed-loop risk management system for the YHJ project.

This study takes the YHJ project as an example, combining risk management theory with project practice, summarizing experience and achieving improvement, forming a closed-loop thinking, which has reference significance for risk assessment and risk response in the subsequent development process of the project.

Key words: Real estate projects；Risk management；Risk identification; Risk assessment；AHP
