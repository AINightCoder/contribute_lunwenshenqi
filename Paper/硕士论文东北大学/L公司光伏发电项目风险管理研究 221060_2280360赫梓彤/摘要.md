# 摘 要

在全球能源转型的大背景下，光伏发电以其清洁、可再生的特性，成为了推动能源结构优化的重要力量。然而，光伏发电项目的实施并非一帆风顺，它面临着技术复杂性、市场环境波动、政策不确定性等多重挑战。这些风险因素不仅可能干扰项目的时间进度与财务预算，还可能损害项目的经济回报和社会影响力。因此，深入研究和识别光伏发电项目的风险因素，对于制定科学的风险管理策略，保障项目的顺利实施和成功运营具有重要意义。因此，本文以 L 公司的光伏发电项目为研究对象，基于项目的风险特性，构建了风险识别模型，并分析了影响项目安全稳定运行的各种风险因素，对项目风险的权重进行了量化。同时，为了有效降低项目风险并确保项目的安全稳定运行，提出了应对风险因素的方法，以保障项目的顺利设计、施工和运营，从而实现经济效益与社会效益的双赢。

研究针对 L 公司的光伏发电项目，通过分析项目风险识别与控制的相关文献资料，结合项目实际情况，初步识别出一系列潜在的风险因素。进一步采用德尔菲法，通过三轮问卷调查，对初步识别的风险因素进行了深入的评估和筛选，确定了包括技术选型风险、财务风险、施工安全风险、成本风险及运维风险在内的 12 项关键风险因素清单。在此框架下，构建了项目风险评价的评价指标，并运用层级分析法对 L 公司光伏发电项目进行了权重分析，基于层次分析法的研究成果对项目开展模糊综合评价，发现该项目的风险因素整体风险水平处于中低水平，其中技术选型风险在筹建筹备阶段凸显为最大风险因素，其评价量化值高达 0.54。其次是建设阶段的成本风险显著上升，评价量化值达到0.5。此外，施工安全风险和质量控制风险也接近中等风险水平，分别评价为0.46和0.48。这两个方面直接关系到项目的安全性和可靠性。

研究结论指出，L公司光伏发电项目在面临多重风险的同时，也具备较高的可行性和稳定性。为确保项目的顺利实施，研究建议项目团队应重点关注技术选型、成本控制、施工安全与运维管理等方面，并制定相应的风险管理措施。同时，研究特别突出了持续风险监测和评估的关键性，旨在保障项目能够在变化莫测的环境下稳定推进。

关键词：光伏发电项目；风险识别；风险评估；层次分析；模糊评价法

# Abstract

In the context of global energy transition, photovoltaic power generation has become an important force to promote the optimization of energy structure due to its clean and renewable characteristics. However, the implementation of photovoltaic power generation projects is not all smooth sailing, and it faces multiple challenges such as technical complexity, market environment fluctuations, and policy uncertainty. These risk factors may not only affect the schedule and cost of the project, but can also have a profound impact on the economic and social impact of the project. Therefore, in-depth research and identification of risk factors of photovoltaic power generation projects is of great significance for formulating scientific risk management strategies to ensure the smooth implementation and successful operation of the project. Therefore, this paper takes the L unit photovoltaic power generation project as the research object, constructs a risk identification model based on the risk characteristics of the project, analyzes various risk factors affecting the safe and stable operation of the project, and quantifies the weight of the project risk. At the same time, in order to effectively reduce the project risk and ensure the safe and stable operation of the project, a method to deal with risk factors is proposed to ensure the smooth design, construction and operation of the project, so as to achieve a win-win situation of economic and social benefits.

In this study, a series of potential risk factors are preliminarily identified by analyzing the relevant literature on project risk identification and control, combined with the actual situation of the project. Furthermore, the Delphi method was used to conduct in-depth evaluation and screening of the preliminarily identified risk factors through three rounds of questionnaire surveys, and a list of 12 key risk factors including technology selection risk, financial risk, construction safety risk, cost risk and operation and maintenance risk was determined. Under this framework, the evaluation index of project risk assessment was constructed, and the weight analysis of L unit photovoltaic power generation project was carried out by using the hierarchical analysis method, and the fuzzy comprehensive evaluation of the project was carried out based on the research results of the analytic hierarchy process, and it was found that the overall risk level of the project was at a medium and low level, and the risk of technology selection was highlighted as the largest risk factor in the preparation stage, and its evaluation quantitative value was as high as 0.54. Secondly, the cost risk in the construction stage has increased significantly, and the quantitative value of evaluation has reached 0.5. In addition, the construction safety risk and quality control risk are also close to the medium risk level, with evaluations of 0.46 and 0.48, respectively. These two aspects are directly related to the safety and reliability of the project.

The conclusion of the study points out that the L unit photovoltaic power generation project has high feasibility and stability while facing multiple risks. In order to ensure the smooth implementation of the project, the study suggests that the project team should focus on technology selection, cost control, construction safety and operation and maintenance management, and formulate corresponding risk management measures. At the same time, the study also highlights the importance of continuous risk monitoring and assessment to ensure that projects move forward in a complex and volatile environment.

Keywords: photovoltaic power generation project; Risk assessment; Analytic Hierarchy Process; Fuzzy evaluation method
