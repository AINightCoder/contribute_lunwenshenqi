# 摘 要

近年来储罐内浮顶倾覆事故频发，储罐安全引发社会关注。相关消防标准规范的变化体现出了对罐区安全、环保要求越来越严格的趋势，促使国内陆续开展对已建成的不满足新标准要求的内浮顶储罐的改造工程。储罐内浮顶改造项目融合了石油化工建设项目所固有的风险与改造项目特有的不确定性风险，展现出工程规模庞大、涉及介质高度危险以及工程实施过程中不确定性因素众多的特征，这些都对项目的风险管理工作构成了严峻挑战。

在储罐内浮顶改造工程广泛实施的背景下，本文以 X 储运公司的储罐内浮顶改造项目作为具体案例，深入探讨了在此类改造过程中遇到的各种风险因素。引入风险管理理论，从风险识别、评估、应对、监控四个维度进行了详尽的研究。在风险识别部分，通过文献调研和现场调查，归纳出了技术、质量、工期、成本、安全、组织、政策七大类别风险。运用德尔菲法，设计并发放了问卷调查，邀请行业内的专家对该改造项目中潜在风险因素进行打分,筛选出项目风险因素。在风险评估部分，运用模糊层次分析法（FAHP）对项目风险因素做出定性定量相结合的评估，确定出各风险因素的权重和等级。在风险应对部分，对不同权重和等级的风险针对性地采取了风险规避、风险缓解策略，提出了具体应对措施。在风险监控部分，建立了项目风险监控的体系和流程，并制定了风险应急处置计划。本文研究发现储罐内浮顶改造项目兼具石油化工建设项目和改造项目的特点，风险因素复杂，将风险管理理论应用于项目风险管理能够保证项目目标顺利完成。

本文的研究拓展了风险管理理论在特定工程项目领域的应用范畴，为风险管理理论提供新的研究应用和实践案例。所研究的项目风险管理成果可以为相关行业提供借鉴和参考。

关键词：储罐改造，项目风险管理，风险评价，风险应对

# Abstract

In recent years, the frequent tipping accidents of internal floating roofs of storage tanks have attracted social attention to tank safety. The changes in relevant fire protection standards and norms reflect the increasingly strict requirements for tank area safety and environmental protection, prompting China to successively carry out renovation projects for internal floating roof storage tanks that do not meet the new standards. The internal floating roof transformation project of storage tanks combines the risks inherent in petrochemical construction projects and the uncertain risks unique to transformation projects, showing characteristics such as large engineering scale, highly dangerous media involved, and numerous uncertain factors during the engineering implementation, which all pose serious challenges to the risk management work of the project.

Under the background of the widespread implementation of internal floating roof transformation projects of storage tanks, this paper takes the internal floating roof transformation project of storage tanks of X Storage and Transportation Company as a specific case to deeply explore the various risk factors encountered in this type of transformation. By introducing the theory of risk management, a detailed study is conducted from four dimensions: risk identification, assessment, response, and monitoring. In the risk identification part, the Delphi method is used to design and distribute questionnaires, and industry experts are invited to score the potential risk factors of this transformation project. Seven major categories of risks, including technology, quality, construction period, cost, safety, organization, and policy, are summarized. In the risk assessment part, the fuzzy analytic hierarchy process (FAHP) is used to determine the weights and levels of project risk factors. In the risk response part, specific response measures are taken for risks of different weights and levels. In the risk monitoring part, the project risk monitoring system and process are established, and the risk emergency response plan is formulated. The research in this paper shows that the internal floating roof transformation project of storage tanks has the characteristics of both petrochemical construction projects and transformation projects, and the risk factors are complex. It is feasible to use the FAHP method to conduct risk management research on the internal floating roof transformation project of storage tanks.

The research in this paper expands the application scope of risk management theory in specific engineering project fields and provides new research applications and practical cases for risk management theory. The project risk management achievements studied can provide references for related industries.

Key words: tank retrofitting; project risk management; risk assessment; risk response
