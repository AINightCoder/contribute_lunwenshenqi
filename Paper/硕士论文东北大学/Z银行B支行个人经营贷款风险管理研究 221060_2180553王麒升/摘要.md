# 摘 要

随着社会主义市场经济的推进和国有企业改革的深入，伴随着相关国家政策的制定，逐渐构建了有利于私营经济健康发展的环境，个体和私营经济对融资的需求日益增长。在这一大背景下，个人经营贷款迎来了发展机遇，并成为商业银行个人经营贷款业务的新的盈利增长点，受到了我国商业银行越来越多的关注。个人经营贷款的余额不断攀升，呈现出迅猛的增长态势。与此同时，由于个人经营贷款业务在我国商业银行中属于新兴业务，截止到目前，仍未针对该业务建立起统一的风险管理准则，也未构建起完整而独立的管理体系，仅作为我国商业银行全面风险管理的一个组成部分存在。因此，有必要建立和不断完善商业银行个人经营贷款的风险管理体系，以适应这一业务不断发展的需要。

本文以 Z 银行B 支行为研究对象，首先对研究背景与意义、文献综述、研究内容与方法进行概述，对商业银行个人经营贷款风险识别、个人经营贷款风险评价、个人经营贷款风险控制研究进行概述，理清信息不对称理论、数字信贷风险控制理论、全面风险管理理论等相关理论，为本研究奠定理论基础。其次，分析Z银行 B 支行个人经营贷款的风险管理现状，用文献回顾法、德尔菲法等方法识别了Z银行 B 支行个人经营贷款风险，并从财务和非财务维度剖析了该行的信用风险。然后选择Z 银行B支行个人经营贷款风险评价模型，识别Z银行B 支行个人经营贷款风险评价模型指标层，进而建立 Z 银行 B 支行个人经营贷款风险评价模型，并选进行应用分析。最后，针对问题从风险控制体系和风险控制流程两个方面进行分析阐述提出了具体的风险控制措施。

本文研究以 Z银行 B 支行为案例，探讨个人经营贷款风险管理的问题。通过本研究的结论和建议，可以帮助该支行改进对个人经营贷款的风险管控，进而提升整体风险管理水平。这有助于提高该支行的盈利能力、客户信任度和市场竞争力。同时，这些研究成果也可能为其他同业机构提供借鉴和指导，促进整个行业风险管理水平的提升。

关键词：个人经营贷款；商业银行；风险管理；风险评估模型

# Abstract

With the advancement of the socialist market economy and the deepening of the reform of state-owned enterprises, along with the formulation of relevant national policies, a favorable environment conducive to the healthy development of private economy has gradually been established. The demand for financing from individuals and private enterprises is increasing. Against this backdrop, personal loans have ushered in a development opportunity, becoming a new profit growth point for commercial banks' personal loan business and attracting more and more attention from commercial banks in China. The balance of personal loans continues to soar, showing a rapid growth trend. At the same time, since personal loan business is still considered an emerging business in Chinese commercial banks, a unified risk management standard has not yet been established for this business, nor has a complete and independent management system been constructed. It only exists as a part of the comprehensive risk management of Chinese commercial banks. Therefore, it is necessary to establish and continuously improve the risk management system for personal loans in commercial banks to meet the evolving needs of this business.

This article takes Z Bank, Branch B as the research object. Firstly, it provides an overview of the research background and significance, literature review, research content and methods, outlining the research on risk identification, evaluation, and control of personal loans in commercial banks. It clarifies relevant theories such as information asymmetry theory, digital credit risk control theory, and comprehensive risk management theory to lay the theoretical foundation for this study. Secondly, it analyzes the current status of personal loan risk management at Z Bank, Branch B, identifies the risks of personal loans at Z Bank, Branch B using methods such as literature review and Delphi method, and analyzes the credit risk of the bank from financial and non-financial dimensions. Then, it selects a risk evaluation model for personal loans at Z Bank, Branch B, identifies the indicators for the risk evaluation model, establishes the risk evaluation model, and conducts application analysis. Finally, specific risk control measures are proposed from the perspectives of risk control system and risk control process to address the issues.

This study uses Z Bank, Branch B as a case study to explore the issues of personal loan risk management. The conclusions and recommendations of this research can help the branch improve its risk control over personal loans, thereby enhancing the overall risk management level. This can help increase the branch's profitability, customer trust, and market competitiveness. At the same time, these research findings may also provide references and guidance for other industry peers, promoting the overall improvement of the industry's risk management level.

Key words: Personal business loans; Commercial banks; Risk management; Risk assessment model
