# 摘 要

经过多年的发展，融资融券交易已经成为了我国证券市场的一项重要交易制度，对于提高市场流动性和稳定性、完善价格发现机制、增强市场风险控制能力等方面都发挥了积极的作用。在单边市场中，投资者只能通过买入或卖出实现盈利，市场缺乏做空机制，导致市场波动较大。而融资融券交易的推出，为市场提供了做空机制，使得投资者可以在市场下跌时也能实现盈利，从而在一定程度上平衡了市场供求关系，降低了市场的波动性和投机性，提高了市场的稳定性。随着上市公司数量的不断增多及科创板、创业板注册制改革的全面铺开，融资融券标的证券和担保品范围也随之迅速扩大。可以说，证券公司融资融券业务风险管理的压力与难度大幅增加。

本文主要以风险管理为研究理论的基础，对 ZT 证券公司融资融券业务的风险控制进行研究。通过对 ZT证券公司现有组织结构以及融资融券业务风险管理体系现状的分析，了解风险管理的相关准则。并通过对专家们和相关工作人员的深入访谈法探索融资融券业务风险管理存在的问题和相关原因分析，通过对风险管理总体水平进行定性和定量相结合的评估，得出公司风险控制处于中等风险水平的结论，虽然公司对风险管理采取一系列控制手段，但仍有待提升。本文对调查研究发现的四类风险提供了适合 ZT 证券公司融资融券业务发展的优化方案。在防范信用风险方面，提出了提高科研队伍实力和建立风险事件数据库等具体的优化措施，以及定量信用评级措施和客户保留率实时监控等优化措施，在防范操作风险方面，提出了集中化管理业务流程和建立科技操作系统的优化措施，在防范流动性风险方面，提出了提升员工应对能力和根据公司流动比率和准备金占比动态调整等优化措施。最后提出了健全两融风险管理体系、定期开展风险监控工作、加强风险审计工作保障措施，为ZT证券公司提升自身融资融券业务风险控制水平提供了参考。

关键词：融资融券业务；风险识别；风险控制

# Abstract

After years of development, margin trading has become an important trading system in China's securities market, playing a positive role in improving market liquidity and stability, improving price discovery mechanisms, and enhancing market risk control capabilities. In a unilateral market, investors can only make profits by buying or selling, and the market lacks a short selling mechanism, resulting in significant market volatility. The launch of margin trading provides a short selling mechanism for the market, allowing investors to achieve profits even when the market falls, thereby balancing market supply and demand to a certain extent, reducing market volatility and speculation, and improving market stability. With the continuous increase in the number of listed companies and the comprehensive implementation of the registration system reform on the Science and Technology Innovation Board and the Growth Enterprise Board, the scope of securities and collateral for margin trading has also rapidly expanded. It can be said that the pressure and difficulty of risk management in securities companies' margin trading have significantly increased.

This article mainly studies the risk control of ZT Securities Company's margin trading business based on risk management theory. By analyzing the existing organizational structure of ZT Securities Company and the current status of the securities lending business, we aim to understand the relevant guidelines for risk management. And through in-depth interviews with experts and relevant staff to explore the problems and trading and securities lending business, classify and summarize the risks faced by the company. Through two methods, Analytic Hierarchy Process and Comprehensive Fuzzy Evaluation, the overall level of risk management is evaluated qualitatively and quantitatively, and the conclusion is drawn that the company's risk control is at a medium risk level, Although the company adopts a series of control measures for risk management, there is still room for improvement. This article provides optimization solutions Company's margin trading business based on the four types of risks identified in the investigation and research. In terms of preventing market risks and credit risks, specific optimization measures such as improving the strength of scientific research teams and establishing risk event databases, as well as quantitative credit rating measures and real-time monitoring of customer retention rates, have been proposed. In terms of preventing operational risks, optimization measures such as centralized management of business processes and the establishment of technological operating systems have been proposed. In terms of preventing liquidity risks, Proposed optimization measures such as improving employees' coping ability and dynamically adjusting the company's liquidity ratio and reserve ratio. Finally, measures were proposed to improve the risk management system for both financing and securities lending, regularly carry out risk monitoring work, strengthen risk audit work, strengthen securities lending business, and improve the construction of personnel team for margin trading and securities lending business, providing reference for ZT Securities Company to improve its risk control level in margin trading and securities lending business.

Key words： Margin trading business； Risk identification； Risk control
