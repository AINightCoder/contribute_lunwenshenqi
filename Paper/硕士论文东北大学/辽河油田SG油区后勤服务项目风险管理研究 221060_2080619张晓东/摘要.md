# 摘要

随着中国经济的不断发展，中国正逐步走进国际舞台的中央，国内各行业快速发展，逐步具备全球视野及能力，行业间的市场格局越来越明确，企业间竞争越来越充分。如何在快速竞争的市场中提高企业的风险应对能力，化解各种不利因素影响，实现企业的经营目标，是关乎企业存亡的关键问题。“三供一业”企业如何应对改革后的新的企业发展需求，如何在重新调整的业务领域开展项目的风险管理，已经成为急需解决的问题。

SG 油区后勤服务项目是 ZX 公司负责的众多后勤服务项目之一，其项目主要内容是在 2021 年 1 月 1 日至 2025 年 12 月 31 日期间内，向 SG 油区驻矿采油厂、工程技术处、车辆服务中心三家单位提供后勤服务。该项目在风险管理中，存在着风险识别不完整、风险管理计划不够完善、治理体系不完善、风险应对预案不充分等问题。本文依据风险管理理论，运用头脑风暴法对该项目进行初步风险识别，形成初始风险清单。结合WBS-RBS 矩阵法，在初步识别结果的基础上进行风险确认，剔除无关风险，整理得到风险清单。探寻了风险来自于财务风险、运行风险、安全风险、人员风险、合规风险、舆论风险六个大类的 20 条风险因素，为后续的风险评估分析奠定了基础。随后使用层次分析法确定指标权重值，再使用模糊综合评价法对项目进行风险评估。从而得出项目处于中等风险水平，并对风险水平由高到低排序，六大类型风险中，安全风险为高风险，运行风险、合规风险为中风险，财务风险、人员风险、舆论风险为较低风险。根据风险评估结果，制定了各项风险指标的应对策略。对高风险优先采取回避和转移的应对策略，中等风险使用减轻的应对策略，低风险尽量使用风险减轻，也可以使用风险接受的应对策略，并给出了风险的应对措施和保障措施。

本研究通过对SG 油区后勤服务项目风险管理进行分析研究，构建后勤服务项目风险管理模型。希望本文中所用到的风险管理方法，能为其他后勤服务项目提供风险管理指导方法和实践经验借鉴。同时对完善我国后勤服务项目风险管理起到了一定的作用。

关键词：后勤服务项目；风险识别；风险评价；风险应对

# Abstract

With the continuous development of the Chinese economy, China is gradually entering the center of the international stage, and various domestic industries are rapidly developing, gradually possessing a global perspective and capabilities. The market pattern between industries is becoming increasingly clear, and competition among enterprises is becoming more and more abundant. How to improve the risk response ability of enterprises in a rapidly competitive market, resolve various adverse factors, and achieve business goals is a key issue related to the survival of enterprises. How to respond to the new development needs of enterprises after the reform, and how to carry out project risk management in the readjusted business field, has become an urgent problem that needs to be solved.

The SG Oil Region Logistics Service Project is one of the numerous logistics service projects undertaken by ZX Company. Its main content is to be responsible for the logistics service business of the SG Oil Region's stationed mining and oil production plant, engineering and technology department, and vehicle service center. In the risk management of this project, there are issues such as inaccurate risk assessment, untimely risk identification, incomplete risk analysis, incomplete risk response strategies, and insufficient risk monitoring. This article is based on risk management theory and uses brainstorming to conduct preliminary risk identification for the project, forming an initial risk list. Based on the preliminary identification results, the WBS-RBS matrix method is used to confirm risks, eliminate irrelevant risks, and compile a risk list. Explored 20 risk factors from six major categories of financial risk, operational risk, security risk, personnel risk, compliance risk, and public opinion risk, laying the foundation for subsequent risk assessment and analysis. Subsequently, the Analytic Hierarchy Process was used to determine the weight of the indicators, and then the fuzzy comprehensive evaluation method was used to evaluate project risks. Thus, it can be concluded that the project is at a higher risk level, and the risk level is ranked from high to low. Among the six types of risks, safety risk is high risk, operational risk and compliance risk are medium risk, and financial risk, personnel risk, and public opinion risk are relatively low risks. Based on the risk assessment results, response strategies for various risk indicators have been developed. Priority should be given to adopting avoidance and transfer strategies for high risks, using mitigation strategies for medium risks, using risk mitigation strategies as much as possible for low risks, or using risk acceptance strategies, and providing risk response and guarantee measures.

This study analyzes and studies the risk management of logistics service projects in SG oil field, and constructs a risk management model for logistics service projects. I hope that the risk management methods used in this article can provide risk management guidance and practical experience for other logistics service projects. At the same time, it has played a certain role in improving the risk management of logistics service projects in China.

Key words: Logistics service project; Risk identification; Risk assessment; Risk response
