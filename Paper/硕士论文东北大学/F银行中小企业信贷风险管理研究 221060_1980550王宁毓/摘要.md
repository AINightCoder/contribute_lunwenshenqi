# 摘 要

2018 年，中国银监会开始对于开展中小企业信贷风险融资做出要求，银监会也在同一时间指出，商业银行作为服务于地区经济发展的金融机构，应当明确中小企业当前在城市、社会、经济发展中做出了突出的贡献，应对其发展提供足够的支持，从而为扶持我国制造业、农林经济行业、新型科技企业等提供便利。政府部门为了促进中小企业的发展陆续出台了多项信贷融资优惠政策予以支持，但由于中小企业本身的经营管理存在一定的弊端、面临的经营环境复杂、抗风险能力较差等，在实际的信贷业务中表现较差，也使得商业银行面临一定的经营风险。在此背景下，研究商业银行如何针对中小企业构建信贷业务的风险识别、评估以及风险应对机制就很有必要。

本文首先应用文献分析法，对有关银行业、金融机构对中小企业信贷风险的相关研究现状等进行归纳，并在此基础上对本文涉及的中小企业、信贷风险的概念、信贷风险的相关理论，以及信贷风险评价的相关概念进行了论述；随后应用调研法，对F银行的相关中小企业信贷业务的项目的具体情况、信贷业务规模，以及针对中小企业信贷风险评估的现状、存在的问题进行分析；接着在前文分析的基础上结合风险管理的流程，进行风险识别，主要识别出经营风险、信用风险、操作风险、战略风险等方面风险；然后应用层次分析方法构建F银行的信贷风险评估模型，并基于这一模型确定相应的权重体系、结合定性分析评估的得分，并确定评估体系的适应性等；最后提出了F 银行的信贷风险应对与控制措施，包括信贷风险的应对策略，以及信贷风险管理的具体应对措施，以及相应的监控措施等。

对银行而言，必须要结合地方的具体情况，建立起完善的信贷风险识别和防控综合管理制度。除了要结合实际情况以外，还需要考虑到地方行业的特征及中小企业的发展状况。必须要做到有针对性的分析，F银行才可以真正做好对于中小企业信贷风险的控制。

关键词：信贷业务；层次分析法；信贷风险；风险应对

# Abstract

In 2018, the China Banking Regulatory Commission began to make requirements for the development of credit risk financing of SME. At the same time, the CBRC pointed out that commercial banks, as financial institutions serving regional economic development, should make it clear that SME have made outstanding contributions to urban, social and economic development, and should provide adequate support for their development. So as to support China's manufacturing industry, agriculture and forestry economic industries, new technology enterprises to provide convenience. In order to promote the development of small and medium-sized enterprises, the government has successively issued a number of preferential credit and financing policies to support them. However, due to certain drawbacks in the operation and management of small and medium-sized enterprises, complex business environment and poor anti-risk ability, they have poor performance in actual credit business, which also makes commercial banks face certain operational risks. In this context, it is necessary to study how commercial banks construct risk identification, assessment and risk response mechanisms for SMEs' credit business.

This paper firstly applies the literature analysis method to summarize the relevant research background, research significance and research status of the banking industry and financial institutions on the credit risk of small and medium-sized enterprises. On this basis, the paper discusses the concept of credit risk, relevant theories of credit risk and related concepts of credit risk evaluation. Then, the research method is applied to analyze the specific situation of F Bank's projects related to SME credit business, the scale of credit business, and the status quo and existing problems of credit risk assessment for SMEs. Then, on the basis of the above analysis, combined with the risk management process, risk identification is carried out, mainly to identify the operational risk, credit risk, operational risk, strategic risk and other aspects of risk; Then, the credit risk assessment model of F bank is constructed by AHP method, and the corresponding weight system is determined based on this model, the score of qualitative analysis and the adaptability of the evaluation system are determined. Finally, the paper puts forward the countermeasures and control measures of $\mathrm { F }$ bank's credit risk, including the countermeasures of credit risk, the specific countermeasures of credit risk management, and the corresponding monitoring measures.

For banks, it is necessary to establish a comprehensive management system for credit risk identification and prevention based on local conditions. In addition to considering the actual situation, it is also necessary to consider the characteristics of local industries and the development status of small and medium-sized enterprises. Targeted analysis is necessary for F Bank to truly control credit risks for small and medium-sized enterprises.

Key words: Credit business; AHP; Credit evaluation; Risk response
