# 摘 要

随着科技的迅猛发展和市场开放程度的深化，近年来游戏行业在中国市场实现了显著的扩张。同时，国际化趋势的加强促进了游戏本地化行业的迅速崛起和成熟，为中国市场培育了众多专业的游戏本地化服务公司。这些公司凭借深厚的市场积淀和优质的服务，推动了游戏本地化的进程，使中国的游戏本地化产业逐渐走向国际舞台。

CC 创新公司顺应这一历史潮流，凭借对市场的敏锐洞察和本地化行业的深刻理解，实现了快速的成长。然而，随着市场逐渐成熟，游戏本地化行业的竞争日趋激烈，CC 创新公司原有粗放的管理模式已难以适应当前的市场需求。中标价格的透明化和压缩、外部合作成本的不断上升，使得公司的盈利能力面临严峻挑战。此外，游戏开发种类的多样化增加了本地化项目的复杂性，项目需求的变化更加频繁，为项目执行带来了诸多不确定性。

本论文以风险管理理论为框架，深入探讨了 CC 创新公司 LX 游戏本地化项目在执行过程中面临的风险。通过访谈法结合头脑风暴法进行细致的分析，识别了项目内部和外部的多种风险因素，如文本难度、项目周期问题、资源分配问题、沟通障碍、技术挑战、以及合作伙伴的不稳定性等。这些风险因素的全面识别为项目风险管理的策略制定提供了重要依据。

为了对该项目的风险进行定性与定量相结合的系统分析，本论文采用了层次分析法（AHP）进行风险评估。通过构建层次结构模型，将复杂的评估问题分解为多个层次和子问题，并对风险类型和风险因素的权重进行系统的评估。层次分析法的应用揭示了各个风险因素对项目成功的影响程度，确定了它们的相对重要性，为风险管理策略的优先级排序提供了科学依据。

在风险识别和评估的基础上，本论文提出了针对 LX 游戏本地化项目的风险应对策略、监控流程和保障措施。其中，风险应对策略包括风险规避、风险预防、风险转移、风险减轻和风险接受；监控流程则涉及定期的风险评估、风险报告和应对计划的调整；保障措施则包括组织架构的优化、项目团队的教育培训、技术保障和应急预案的制定等。

本论文旨在为 CC 创新公司 LX 游戏本地化项目的风险管理提供理论支持和实践指导，在竞争激烈的市场环境中，有效的风险管理对于企业的稳定发展和行业的健康进步具有至关重要的意义。通过论文的深入分析和实践验证，期望能为游戏本地化行业提供一个有效的风险管理模型，促进整个行业风险管理水平的提升，推动行业的持续发展和创新。

关键词：游戏本地化项目；风险管理；风险识别；风险评估

# Abstract

With the rapid development of technology and the deepening of market openness, the game industry has achieved remarkable expansion in the Chinese market in recent years. At the same time, the strengthening of the internationalization trend has facilitated the rapid rise and maturity of the game localization industry, which has nurtured many professional game localization service companies in the Chinese market. With profound market accumulation and high quality services, these companies have promoted the process of game localization and made China's game localization industry gradually move towards the international stage.

CC Innovation has followed this historical trend and achieved rapid growth with its keen insight into the market and deep understanding of the localization industry. However, as the market gradually matures and the competition in the game localization industry becomes more and more intense, CC Innovation's original loose management model is difficult to adapt to the current market demand. The transparency and compression of the winning price and the rising cost of external cooperation have made the company's profitability face severe challenges. In addition, the diversification of game development types has increased the complexity of localization projects, and the changes in project requirements are more frequent, bringing many uncertainties to project execution.

Taking risk management theory as a framework, this thesis thoroughly explores the risks faced by CC Innovation's LX game localization project in the execution process. Through careful analysis by interview method combined with brainstorming method, a variety of risk factors both internal and external to the project were identified, such as text difficulty, project cycle problems, resource allocation problems, communication barriers, technical challenges, and partner instability. The comprehensive identification of these risk factors provides an important basis for the development of project risk management strategies.

In order to analyze the risks quantitatively and qualitatively, this dissertation adopts the Analytical Hierarchy Process (AHP) for risk assessment. By constructing a hierarchical model, the complex decision-making problem is decomposed into multiple levels and sub-problems, and the weights of risk types and risk factors are systematically evaluated. The application of hierarchical analysis reveals the degree of influence of each risk factor on the success of the project, determines their relative importance, and provides a scientific basis for the prioritization of risk management strategies.

On the basis of risk identification and assessment, this thesis proposes risk response strategies, monitoring processes and safeguards for LX game localization projects. Among them, the risk response strategy includes risk avoidance, risk prevention, risk transfer, risk mitigation and risk acceptance; the monitoring process involves regular risk assessment, risk reporting and adjustment of response plan; and the safeguard measures include optimization of organizational structure, education and training of the project team, technological safeguard and formulation of contingency plan.

This thesis aims to provide theoretical support and practical guidance for the risk management of CC Innovation's LX game localization project. In the competitive market environment, effective risk management is of vital significance for the stable development of enterprises and the healthy progress of the industry. Through the indepth analysis and practical verification of the thesis, it is expected to provide an effective risk management model for the game localization industry, promote the improvement of the risk management level of the whole industry, and push forward the sustainable development and innovation of the industry.

Keywords: Game localization projects; Risk management; Risk identification; Risk assessment
