# 摘 要

近年来，随着互联网的急速发展，洗钱犯罪变得愈发猖獗，由于互联网便捷了资金的流转，让洗钱犯罪变得愈发隐蔽、多样，洗钱途径更加多样化，加大了资金的追踪难度。金融机构的反洗钱履职能力正在遭受着严峻考验，国际反洗钱形势同样严峻。另外，随着电子支付的流行，越来越多的银行产品、第三方支付产品为不法分子提供了洗钱的渠道，银行对客户参与洗钱犯罪的监管难度进一步加大，极大增加了银行对客户的洗钱风险进行管理的难度，阻碍了商业银行的稳健经营。

论文基于Z银行S分行的实际情况，以其个人客户为研究对象，探究了Z 银行S分行的客户洗钱风险管理机制。首先，对政策及国内外研究现状进行梳理，并对研究中涉及的相关概念进行归纳总结，确定研究所需的理论基础。其次，概括其风险管理现状，归纳总结其个人客户洗钱风险管理情况，并对其个人客户洗钱风险管理的流程和内容进行梳理。再次，通过文献研究及数据分析，对个人客户洗钱风险因素进行初步识别，运用德尔菲法对符合Z银行S 分行个人客户实际情况的风险因素进行筛选、识别，运用层次分析法及模糊综合评价法，确定风险因素所占权重，进行层次总排序，并对Z银行S 分行个人客户洗钱风险进行打分，对评价结果进行分析，为后续提出风险控制对策提供依据。最后，结合风险识别、风险评价的结果，从严控客户身份识别风险、提升客户风险监测与评估能力、建立完备的客户风险控制策略、做好技术保障工作、加强宣传引导等五个维度提出针对性风险控制对策。

通过对Z银行S分行个人客户洗钱风险管理进行研究，有助于Z银行S 分行进一步完善自身的洗钱风险管理模型，做好自身的洗钱风险评价，提升自身的洗钱风险控制策略，在今后的发展过程中，能够更好地应对客户洗钱风险管理问题。另外，对于同业机构，可以提供一定的参考，帮助同业机构建立更加完备的客户洗钱风险管理体系。

关键词：洗钱；反洗钱；风险管理；个人客户；银行

# Abstract

In recent years, with the rapid development of the Internet, the crime of money-laundering has become more and more rampant, as the Internet facilitates the flow of funds, making the crime of money-laundering more and more hidden and diverse, and the channels of money-laundering are more diversified, making it more difficult to trace the funds. The ability of financial institutions to perform anti-money laundering duties is being severely tested, and the international anti-money laundering situation is equally severe. In addition, with the popularity of electronic payment, more and more banking products and third-party payment products provide channels for lawbreakers to launder money, and the difficulty for banks to supervise customers involved in money laundering crimes has further increased, greatly increasing the difficulty for banks to manage the risk of money laundering of customers and hindering the sound operation of commercial banks.

Based on the actual situation of Bank Z Branch S, the thesis explores the customer money laundering risk management mechanism of Bank Z Branch S with its individual customers as the research object. First, the policy and the current status of domestic and international research are sorted out, and the relevant concepts involved in the research are summarized to determine the theoretical basis needed for the study. Secondly, it outlines the current situation of its risk management, summarizes its personal customer money laundering risk management, and combs its personal customer money laundering risk management process and content. Once again, through literature research and data analysis, the preliminary identification of personal customer money laundering risk factors, the use of the Delphi method to meet the actual situation of the personal customers of Bank Z Branch S risk factors for screening, identification, the use of hierarchical analysis and fuzzy comprehensive evaluation method, to determine the weighting of risk factors, to carry out the hierarchical total sorting, and scoring of personal customer money laundering risk of Bank Z Branch S. The evaluation results are analyzed to provide a basis for the subsequent proposal of risk control and risk management. The evaluation results are analyzed to provide a basis for the subsequent proposal of risk control countermeasures. Finally, combining the results of risk identification and risk evaluation, it puts forward targeted risk control countermeasures in five dimensions, such as strictly controlling the risk of customer identification, improving the ability of customer risk monitoring and assessment, establishing a complete customer risk control strategy, doing a good job of technical protection, and strengthening publicity and guidance.

Through the study of money laundering risk management of individual customers of Bank Z Branch S, it helps Bank Z Branch S to further improve its own money laundering risk management model, do a good job of its own money laundering risk evaluation, and improve its own money laundering risk control strategy, so that it can better cope with customer money laundering risk management problems in the future development process. In addition, for peer organizations, it can provide certain reference to help peer organizations establish a more complete customer money laundering risk management system.
